Welcome to Multi-Source Article Synthesizer

What We Do

Multi-Source Article Synthesizer is a news aggregation platform that brings together articles from multiple trusted sources including The Guardian, Currents API and GDELT. Our AI-powered system groups similar stories from different sources and creates comprehensive summaries, giving you a complete picture of the news from multiple perspectives.

How It Works

  1. Browse by Category: Click on any topic in the navigation menu (World, U.S., Business, Technology, Sports, Entertainment, Science, Health, or Politics) to see the latest news grouped by story.
  2. Search for Topics: Use the search bar to find articles about any topic. Our system searches across all news sources simultaneously.
  3. Read AI Summaries: Each story group includes a comprehensive AI-generated summary that synthesizes information from multiple sources, giving you a complete understanding without reading every article.
  4. Explore Multiple Sources: Click on any article to read the full story from its original source. See how different news outlets cover the same story.

Get started by browsing a category above or searching for a topic that interests you!
